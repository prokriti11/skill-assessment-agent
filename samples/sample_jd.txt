Senior Backend Engineer
TechScale Inc. | Full-Time | Remote-First

About TechScale:
TechScale is a Series B fintech startup building next-generation payment infrastructure for emerging markets. We process $2B+ in transactions annually across 15 countries. Our engineering team is 40 engineers strong and growing fast.

About the Role:
We're looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Platform team. You'll design and build the core services that power our payment processing pipeline — low-latency, high-availability systems that millions of transactions flow through daily.

Responsibilities:
- Design, build, and maintain high-throughput microservices in Python (FastAPI/Flask) and occasionally Go
- Own the reliability and scalability of our payment processing APIs (SLA: 99.99%)
- Lead technical design reviews and architecture decisions for the Platform team
- Build and optimize PostgreSQL and Redis data layers; design schemas for financial data
- Implement async task processing with Celery and message queuing with Kafka
- Integrate with third-party payment providers (Stripe, Razorpay, SWIFT) and ensure PCI-DSS compliance
- Write and maintain comprehensive unit, integration, and load tests (pytest, Locust)
- Mentor 2-3 junior engineers; conduct code reviews with an eye for security and performance
- Participate in on-call rotation for production systems; lead incident postmortems
- Collaborate with product and data teams to expose clean internal APIs

Requirements (Must Have):
- 4+ years of backend engineering experience
- Strong Python proficiency — you can write production-grade async Python (asyncio, FastAPI or Django REST)
- Deep understanding of relational databases — PostgreSQL specifically (query optimization, indexing strategies, transactions, EXPLAIN ANALYZE)
- Redis — caching patterns, TTL strategies, Pub/Sub, distributed locking
- RESTful API design — you've built and versioned APIs consumed by multiple teams
- System design fundamentals — you can design for horizontal scaling, handle race conditions, think about failure modes
- Distributed systems concepts — CAP theorem, eventual consistency, idempotency in payment systems
- Git workflows, CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions or similar)
- Docker and containerization — you've shipped services in containers
- Strong written and verbal communication — you write design docs and can explain trade-offs clearly

Nice to Have:
- Experience with Kafka or RabbitMQ (message queuing at scale)
- Go experience (we have services in Go and are expanding)
- AWS or GCP cloud services (RDS, ElastiCache, ECS, Lambda)
- Kubernetes — you've deployed to a k8s cluster
- Experience in fintech, payments, or highly regulated environments
- Knowledge of PCI-DSS compliance requirements

What We Offer:
- Competitive compensation: ₹28-45 LPA based on experience
- Fully remote with quarterly offsites
- Learning budget of ₹75,000/year
- 25 days PTO + company holidays
